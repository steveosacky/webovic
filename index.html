The Story

Inspired by a man of many talents, Webovic 2.0 is the resurgence of a timeless classic virtual board game that first appeared on the world wide web in the 1990s.

This game is reserved for the most interesting of minds, those who seek to stay weird, those white and nerdy, and those who ain't nothin'.
